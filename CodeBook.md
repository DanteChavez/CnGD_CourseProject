## Activity Labels

* `WALKING`: When value is `1` the subject was walking during the test.
* `WALKING_UPSTAIRS`: When value is `2` subject was walking up a staircase during the test.
* `WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS`: When value is `3` the subject was walking down a staircase during the test.
* `SITTING`: When value is `4` the subject was sitting during the test.
* `STANDING`: When value is `5` the subject was standing during the test.
* `LAYING`: When value is `6` the subject was laying down during the test.
